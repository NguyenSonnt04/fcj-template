**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

**Week 1:** [Getting familiar with AWS and basic AWS services](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [IAM roles, S3, RDS, and highly available architectures](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Server and database migration to AWS](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Monitoring, security, CI/CD, and AWS cost optimization](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Microservices, serverless, and distributed systems on AWS](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Docker, EKS, Fargate, and container CI/CD](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Data Lakes, ETL/ELT, Data Governance, and QuickSight](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [AI/ML, Generative AI, MLOps, and Responsible AI](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [TrustBite onboarding and development environment setup](1.9-week9/)
